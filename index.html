About Us

At Henry's Restaurant, we bring the heart and soul of Vietnam to your plate. Nestled in the heart of the city, our cozy and inviting space is the perfect spot to experience the rich, aromatic flavors of traditional Vietnamese cooking. From the comforting warmth of phở to the crisp, fresh rolls of gỏi cuốn, every dish is crafted with passion, authenticity, and a touch of home. Whether you're a longtime lover of Vietnamese cuisine or trying it for the first time, Henry’s promises a culinary journey you won’t forget. Come hungry, leave happy! That’s the Henry’s way.
